Frequently Asked Questions

Is EasyImageFlex completely free?

Yes, EasyImageFlex is 100% free to use with no hidden charges, watermarks, or limitations. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.

Are my images safe and private?

Absolutely! All image processing happens directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

Does resizing reduce image quality?

Resizing can affect quality, but EasyImageFlex uses smart algorithms to minimize quality loss. You can control the quality level with our quality slider to find the perfect balance.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP, and SVG formats for input. You can also convert between these formats when downloading your processed image.

Can I process multiple images at once?

Yes! Our Convert tool supports bulk processing. You can upload multiple images and convert them all at once to your desired format.

Is there a file size limit?

We recommend images under 10MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process but will still work.
